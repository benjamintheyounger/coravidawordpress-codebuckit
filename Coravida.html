How to arrive at Coravida!

The town of Quepos is conveniently located 5 minutes away, with a modern Marina and beautifully updated boardwalk, where you can go for a nice walk day and night. Quepos has supermarkets and stores for nearly anything you might need.. and for more advanced requests the city of San Jose is just a short 25 min flight from the Quepos airport, or a 3 hour drive.

How to reach us!


From the central Quepos, follow the 618 road towards Manuel Antonio. As you proceed out of Quepos, the road bends sharply to the right and up hill. Turn left and you will see a fountain and a yellow barrier. Once you are at the guard gate, ask for Villa Jacana or Villa Manakin, they are side by side. If you have any trouble finding Manuel Antonio Estates just ask a taxi driver in Quepos, They usually all know how to get there.
